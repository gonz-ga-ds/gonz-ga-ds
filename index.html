Experiments and causal Inference project

Chess Banter: Does engaging with an opponent in online chess impact their performance?
Bullying in online games is a serious problem and online chess is no exception. The purpose of trash talking is to try to decrease opponent performance by distracting opponents into heightened emotions and less logical decision making. This project posits that encountering a text designed to provoke a challenge from an opponent may create a sense of perceived rivalry, ultimately enhancing their gameplay accuracy and performance.
